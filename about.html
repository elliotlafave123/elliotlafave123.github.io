About me

I am a web designer who is starting up a web design business, I have made websites in the past as a hobby or for fun and now want to see if I can make it my future. I am a computer science student in high school and looking to study this further in college; I am looking for new clients who are willing to use my service and help me get my name out there.

I hope to go on to work at a large web development company such as Google or would like to expand my own business to have multiple clients and people working here. I decided to start this company when I realised that there is a real gap in the market where low-cost web development for small businesses is just not available for most people. I will work hard to change this.

My goal is to make the Internet accessible to all small businesses, I hope to do this by providing a high quality service at an affordable price. My service will allow your business to look more professional therefore increasing brand recognition and trust from your potential customers. I hope if you are reading this you get an idea of the type of business i'm trying to create and feel free to Contact Us for more information or Get Started today!
