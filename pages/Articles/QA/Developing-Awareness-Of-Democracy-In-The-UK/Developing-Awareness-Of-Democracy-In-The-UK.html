Developing Awareness of Democracy in the UK | SG

Elliot La Fave

·

08/07/2022

·

15:33pm

How could this work in the UK?

This type of government would not work in the modern age as democracy is viewed by almost everybody as the best way to run a country and to make decisions.

Having a single person or group of people running the country just because they believe that their views/ideas of how to run the country are better doesn’t make for a free and open society.

Having philosophers running the country would be similar to when the royal family was in control of the country. This form of government usually leads to revolts/civil wars within the country in which they rule.

What difference would this make to the NHS?

This could make a difference to the NHS, but it would depend on exactly who’s in charge and what they believe in.

If they see the benefits of the NHS, they would likely pour more funding into the organization and therefore improve patient care.

On the other hand, they might be against the idea of free healthcare and scrap the NHS altogether.

What would this mean for our class system?

The class system would remain the same other than the fact that the philosophers who are in charge would be automatically placed at the top of the class system with the rest of the country below.

There would be no fairness within the government and no hope of normal citizens ever being involved in political processes somewhat like in North Korea where the communist party always controls the country.

Would things be better or worse?

I believe if our government was run in this way the country would be much worse off. This is because having a group of people running a country who all have the same views and ideals is a recipe for civil war and anger within the population.

Also, the fact there are no democratic elections and no way to influence the government would leave citizens wanting to leave the country for a place with real freedom such as America.

Allowing a country to be run in this way could end up destroying the rights of the people, also by this logic you wouldn’t have the public serving on a jury, you would have a jury full of judges, and so on…
