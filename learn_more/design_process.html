Our Design Process


The 7 Step Process

Step 1: Goal identification
We will firstly collect all the infomation we need about your business, we will use this to create a site that works best for you and fits the exact needs of your business.

At this stage we will examine what the site is for, what is expected to be found on the website. The primary aim of the website and what the final result should look like

You and the designer will agree on a checklist of things the website must have in order to fit your criteria.

Step 2: Scope definition
We will create a chart of how we will develop your site and the timeframe in which it will be built and published, this is a good refrence for designers and also for you to keep an eye on progress. A Gantt chart and a contract will be created at this stage.

Step 3: Sitemap and wireframe creation
A sitemap and wireframe of your website will be created to give you a visual representation of how all the pages will link up together.

Step 4: Content creation
Once the websites basic framework is in place we will start adding content to your website. Content serves two essential purposes:

First, content engages readers and drives them to take the actions necessary to fulfill a site’s goals.

Content also boosts a site’s visibility for search engines. The practice of creation and improving content to rank well in search is known as search engine optimization, or SEO.

Step 5: Visual elements
Finally, it’s time to create the visual style for the site. This part of the design process will often be shaped by existing branding elements, colour choices, and logos, as stipulated by the client. But it’s also the stage of the web design process where a good web designer can really shine. Images are taking on a more significant role in web design now than ever before. Not only do high-quality images give a website a professional look and feel, but they also communicate a message, are mobile-friendly, and help build trust.

Step 6: Testing
Once the site has all it's visuals and content we can begin the testing phase. We will ensure that all the links work perfectly and that it loads properly on all devices and browsers.

Step 7: Launch
Now it’s time for everyone’s favorite part of the web design process: When everything has been thoroughly tested, and you’re happy with the site, it’s time to launch.
