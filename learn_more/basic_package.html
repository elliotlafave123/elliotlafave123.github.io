Basic Package

What you get:

1 to 1 E-mail between you and the web designer
Our specialist web developers will have contact with you to decide exactly how to build your site to fit your needs and the needs of your customers

Your website designed to your exact description
Just ask us how you want your pages to look and feel and our developers will do all the rest for you, with this package your site can have up to 4 pages (more can be requested just contact us.)

Weekly updates on progress of your website
We will show you visual updates of the progress in building your site weekly. This will be done by E-mail to the address you provided.

Your site up online in as little as 28 days
Your website will be online for the world to see within 28 days. We will ensure you are satisfied with your website before asking for full payment.

Free .tk domain name provided with your site
We provide free .tk domains to all our customers who do not want to pay monthly fees for a .com or .co.uk domain name. If you would prefer this either contact us or put this in your Request Form.

Google Analytics account to see who visits your site
You can recieve in depth insights on who is visiting your website, when and where from. This will allow you to get a deeper understanding of the sort of people that are drawn to your business to help you to target your advertisments to the right consumers.
